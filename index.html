Designing for Self-Perception of Aging in Low-Resource Settings: Leveraging Reflection, AI, and Cultural Contexts in HCI

A hands-on session using Self-Perception of Aging (SPA) as an entry point to co-design IKS-inspired well-being toolkits across digital, tangible, and hybrid media.

Overview

What this workshop is about

How can SPA, culture and spirituality, and equitable AI be translated into interactive tools for well-being? Building on research with older adults in India and a working SPA reflection app, we will experience IKS-inspired guidance and materialize it into design concepts and low-fi toolkits.

FAQ

What will I make in the workshop?

A low-fi concept for a well-being toolkit (for example, a digital interaction, tangible device, ritual guide, or hybrid service) grounded in IKS-inspired guidance.

Do I need prior knowledge of IKS or SPA?

No. We will provide short primers and design prompts.

What do you do with the outputs?

With consent, we may analyze anonymized artifacts for a post-workshop academic write-up.
